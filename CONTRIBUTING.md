# Contributing to This Book

Thank you for your interest in improving *Power BI for M&E and Public Health Data Analysts*.

This book is an independent work and is actively maintained by the author. Contributions are welcome, but all changes are reviewed carefully to preserve clarity, accuracy, and consistency.

## What Contributions Are Welcome

- Typo corrections
- Clarifying explanations
- Minor improvements to examples
- Suggestions for additional references
- Reporting broken links or rendering issues

## What Contributions Are Not Accepted

- Major rewrites of chapters
- Changes that alter the pedagogical structure
- Content that introduces organization- or donor-specific branding
- Changes that conflict with the book’s scope or audience

## How to Contribute

1. Click **“Edit this page”** or open an issue.
2. For small fixes, submit a pull request.
3. For larger suggestions, open an issue first for discussion.

## Review Process

All pull requests are reviewed by the author.
Acceptance depends on:
- Alignment with the book’s goals
- Clarity and correctness
- Relevance to M&E and public health practice


Thank you for helping improve this resource.
